I am a PhD student in Statistics at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Manchester under the supervision of Korbinian Strimmer.

My current research interest focus on topics between Bayesian statistics and machine learning. I am motivated by techniques for handling the missing data, variable selection methods and algorithms for high-dimensional statistical learning.
